Research Experience

Under the guidance of Prof. Prasanna Kumar S. Mural, I am developing smart gloves with piezoelectric sensors for sign language recognition. The project involves designing and 3D printing a shape-adaptive PVDF polymer sensor that leverages triboelectric properties for joint motion and tactile sensing. An Arduino microcontroller interfaces with the sensor, enabling real-time signal recording, processing, and data logging.

The recorded signals undergo advanced signal processing with a moving window mechanism to construct an input matrix. A hybrid LSTM-Transformer model enhances gesture recognition accuracy, integrating AI-driven analysis with innovative sensor technology for real-time human-machine interaction and assistive applications.
